# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all members to treat each other with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior That Creates a Positive Environment
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct unbecoming of a professional
- Any form of discrimination

## Enforcement

- Community members who violate this code may be temporarily or permanently banned
- The maintainers reserve the right to remove comments or contributions
- Repeated violations will result in account suspension

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. Contact the maintainers directly
2. Email: conduct@area-platform.com
3. All reports will be handled confidentially
